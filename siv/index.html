About

Broadcast Studio Operation

SonicInVision, Inc. provides the New York TV JAPAN Studios with 24 hour, 7 days a week broadcast service and support. Services include Japanese programs Satellite Down link, using automation system for TV program Transmission control, Daily scheduled programming, Video Server, Monitoring shows, Video and Audio editing. Experienced video operators and directors work around the clock to provide TV JAPAN programming to all subscribers in the USA and Canada. Also SIV re-transmits NHK WORLD JAPAN signal 24/7 with Closed Caption for US viewers.

NHK WORLD JAPAN NEWSLINE Live News Production

SonicInVision, Inc. provides NHK World Japan daily flagship newscast Newsline Live production and technical service from a brand new NYC studios.

News Coverage, Contents Production

SonicInVision, Inc. can help guide a client from initial concepts to project completion, including scriptwriting, video shooting, ENG, video editing, music composition and recording, voice over, audio mixdown, duplication, all post and full production services. We pride ourselves on our personal relationships with our clients, and will work with a client’s budget and time constraints.
